# Encapsulation vs Abstraction

## Encapsulation

Encapsulation means hiding data and allowing access through methods.

Example:
In WhatsApp, your phone number can be kept private and accessed using settings. This protects the data.

## Abstraction

Abstraction means hiding complex implementation and showing only important features.

Example:
On Instagram, when we upload a photo, we simply click the Upload button. We do not see the internal coding and processing happening in the background.

## Difference

- Encapsulation focuses on data security.
- Abstraction focuses on hiding complexity.
